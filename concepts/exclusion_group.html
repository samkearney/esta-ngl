Exclusion Group¶

This section is a work-in-progress and should not be referenced at this time.

An exclusion group represents a group of interface instances, identified by their id, of which only one can be used at a time.

Exclusion groups have no attributes. They have one or more children, which are elements which identify an interface instance.

Markup Example¶

<exclusiongroup>
  <interfaceref id="gobo-wheel-1-indexed" />
  <interfaceref id="gobo-wheel-1-continuous" />
</exclusiongroup>
