ESTA Uniform Device Representation (UDR)Â¶

Next Generation Entertainment Control Model: Uniform Device Representation is a method for describing controllable devices in a machine-readable way. UDR is developed by the Entertainment Services and Technology Association (ESTA) and has applications in entertainment technology, architectural lighting and building automation, and the Internet of Things.
